Non-destructive evaluation & testing.

Frasco offers NDE and NDT using the latest, most modern methods. Eddy current is one of the most cost-effective means of evaluating equipment — detecting surface flaws and hairline fractures in conductive materials with minimal preparation, and no need to remove paint or coatings.
