// NDE / NDT

No coating removal. Results the same day.

Eddy current testing is one of the most cost-effective means of evaluating equipment — detecting surface flaws and hairline fractures with minimal preparation. Portable equipment means instant on-site results.

  • No need to remove paint or surface coatings
  • Detects flaws in ferrous and non-ferrous materials
  • Identifies metal grade, hardness, near-surface defects
  • Same-day reporting for fast response to any findings

Prevent failure before it happens.

Equipment breakdown carries an extremely high cost. Frasco partners with your business to ensure minimal downtime — the right evaluation method for every situation, with eddy current testing the most cost-effective and elegant solution for many applications.

  • Chiller, condenser and evaporator tubes
  • Heat exchangers, fin fans, boiler bundles
