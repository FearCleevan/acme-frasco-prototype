NDE / NDT

No coating removal. Same-day findings.

Eddy current testing is one of the most cost-effective means of evaluating equipment — detecting surface flaws and hairline fractures with minimal preparation. Portable, instantaneous, and delivering same-day reporting to keep your facility moving.

  • Works without removing paint or surface coatings
  • Detects flaws in ferrous and non-ferrous materials
  • Identifies metal grade, hardness, and near-surface defects
  • Portable — ideal for on-site plant inspection
  • Certified to CAN/CGSB-48.9712
